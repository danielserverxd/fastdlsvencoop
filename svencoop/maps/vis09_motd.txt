HALF-LIFE: VISITORS
===================
Version 1.0
by Organic700


You play as the young lawyer Jd. Alan Green, who was sent to the desert as a safety evaluator to carry out a safety report of Black Mesa.
After a brief walk towards your temporary office you suddenly find yourself in a situation you are not prepared for, grounds for an automatic fail on the safety evaluation.

Can you use what you have around you to escape this nightmare?
