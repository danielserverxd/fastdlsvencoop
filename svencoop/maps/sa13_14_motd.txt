Mission:  SA13 Sabotage
Location:  Bioterrorist base of operations, Pohnpei island, Micronesia
Source:  Sven Viking
===================================================================

You have to infiltrate on foot beyond the enemy lines and reach the mountain top where we believe something dark is brewing.

There's an unused sewer line that leads to a nearby town in the woods. A chopper will be waiting for you there.


Objectives:

*   Advance through the last enemy lines

*	Find the unused sewer line

*	Find the friendly chopper

*	Reach the mountain top
